Defeating WhatsApp Hijacking

Scammers call pretending to verify a package or vaccination status. They ask for a '6-digit registration code'. Sharing this allows them to instantly lock you out and duplicate your profile.

Shield Action

Enable Two-Step Verification inside Settings > Account > Two-Step Verification. Establish an offline verification PIN.

Easypaisa & JazzCash Security

Fraudulent actors masquerade support channels using standard 11-digit mobile lines, inducing panic regarding 'accidental transfers' to manipulate users into sharing access passcodes.

Shield Action

No verified customer support team will ever request your payment passcodes, transactional OTPs, or validation secrets over any phone line.
