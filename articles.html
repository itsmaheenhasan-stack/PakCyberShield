The Psychology Behind Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks are not only technical attacks. They rely heavily on psychology. Attackers create urgency, fear and trust to manipulate users into making unsafe decisions.

Research Methodology

This study analyzes common phishing patterns and examines how social engineering techniques influence human behavior.

Key Findings

  • Urgency reduces careful decision making
  • Fake authority increases trust
  • Users often ignore verification steps
