Cyber Safety Learning Hub

WhatsApp Security

Common attacks:

• Fake OTP requests
• Fake WhatsApp support accounts
• Suspicious links

Protection:
✓ Enable two-step verification
✓ Never share verification codes
✓ Verify before clicking links

Mobile Payment Safety

Common scams:

• Fake Easypaisa/JazzCash agents
• Prize scams
• Fake payment confirmations

Always verify transactions yourself.

Password & Privacy Protection

Use unique passwords. Enable 2FA. Check app permissions. Avoid sharing personal information publicly.
